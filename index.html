Mr Boss Rice

Here is life history of Mr Boss Rice:

Mr Boss Rice hails from Benue state, Nigeria. He was born into the family of Mr/Mrs Atule and he is the first child of his parent.
He obtained Bachelor Degree in Agricultural Extension in Federal university of Agriculture, Jos. Mr Boss Rice was married to Mrs Mary Rice
and they had five (5) children (2 boys and 3 girls) before he had an accident which almost caused his life in 1998.

Mr Boss Rice was a man of his words, so honest and upright in his dealings with people.
Even on his sick bed he remained a caring and loving father and husband.
He saved millions of life by offering them opportunity to work in his Rice Farm so as to
care for themselves and thier family.
Mr Boss Rice set a legacy for himself and family which he will be remembered for.
Mr Boss Rice died at the age of 89.
